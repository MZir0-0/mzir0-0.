About Us

Biove is a company created to protect pharmaceutical companies and ordinary people from counterfeiting. Using the latest blockchain and nft technologies. Biove is a closed, secure network that provides companies with the ability to monitor their products and give them confidence that their products will not be counterfeited. Also, the company does not forget about the ordinary user, we have developed a convenient system of QR-codes that allows any person to verify the originality of his medicines.Biove is a tool that in the hands of a smart person becomes a weapon to destroy counterfeit

Technology

Using Biove, each pharmacy company connects to their own Ethereum blockchain, allowing them to simultaneously process JSON requests and control network members. Biove uses the ERC-721 smart contract on the Ethereum network to make each certificate unique. This allows you to drive the possibility of forgery to 0. It is also possible to connect other network participants to the AWS Managed Blockchain, for example, the logistics companies you cooperate with, by connecting the AWS Location Service it becomes possible to request the location of products.
